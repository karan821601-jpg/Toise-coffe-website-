The Roastery

Small batch, by design — not by accident.

Toise started as a simple bet: that a neighbourhood café in Worli could roast its own beans and still feel like somewhere you'd duck into on a whim after a walk by the sea.

The upstairs seating is tight — mind your step, mind your head — but that's part of the charm. It's a place built for lingering over a pour-over, not rushing through a queue.
